CUMMINS COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING FOR WOMEN NAGPUR

Cummins College of Engineering for Women is the top Engineering College for Women in Nagpur, & becoming the Best Engineering College in Nagpur.Cummins College of Engineering for Women at Nagpur Established on 27th Feb 2010 & started with funding of Rs. 8 Crores from Cummins India Foundation specifically to empower women of Vidarbha region of Maharashtra.Our Motto is Sharpening Engineering Acumen of women with a Difference. The college is approved by AICTE, New Delhi, and Government of Maharashtra, affiliated to RTM Nagpur University. The College has Management Leadership of Shri Milindji Kukde who is retired Execuitive Engineer from MSEB and has dynamic leadership
